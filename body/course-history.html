Junior Engineering Clinic I

  • What is clinic?
  • Summary:
    I worked in a clinic group which is developing a system which creates arugmented reality using structured light (ARSL for short) using MATALB. The goal to is perform 3D reconstructions of objects, track their movement (in a video), and edit that video with realisitic AR. Existing systems which perform these operations require two cameras, but the system we are developing requires one camera and one projector. At the end of the semester, were wer able to perform a 3D reconstruction of a still image and track 2D points in a video. Our future goals include tracking 3D points and porting to mobile (Android).

Freshman Engineering Clinic II

  • What is clinic?
  • Summary:
    In this course, we studied error in measurement. For the final project, my team disassembled a shopvac and mounted the functional components on the lid of a 5 gallon bucket, so that the vacuum could be used to fill any generic bucket. Several factors of performance were measured before and after modification. Performance of the modified vacuum was found to not be statistically different than that of the original.
